DKU campus redesign plan

This project focuses on redesigning an unused area on the DKU campus. After reviewing a large amount of literature, we created hand-drawn redesign plans and used QGIS to map and synchronize the spatial data.

Game in Processing

I created this game using Processing. Players use the keyboard to control the cat, collect fish, and avoid falling trash. The game is designed to be lighthearted, engaging, and slightly challenging, giving players a sense of satisfaction as they successfully gather food while dodging obstacles.
